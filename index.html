About Me

My name is Leo and I reside in Kearney, MO. I have a passion for utilizing technology to help businesses run more efficiently. This is what I try to do at KC Store Fixtures, where I am Netsuite Administrator and VP of Information Technology. Netsuite is a cloud-based ERP system and I have had great success in customizing it to match our business processes (or vice versa in some cases). To accomplish these improvements I have used: SuiteScript (built on JavaScript), PHP, SQL, and Web Services. I like to do the problem-solving and process re-engineering without any specific technology in mind, then go figure out the best solution.

In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my 1 year old son (Leo V!) and wife, playing and watching sports, video games, reading biographies, watching documentaries, and playing pool. Favorite TV show is The Wire and I have fully watched the series LOST more than once. Favorite hobby in the world - Ultimate Frisbee.

I am a proud alum of the University of Missouri, where I graduated with a BS in Industrial Engineering and MBA.
